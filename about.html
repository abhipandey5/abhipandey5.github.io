About Me

I am Abhishek Kumar Pandey, a PhD Scholar at the Center for Security, Theory and Algorithmic Research (C-STAR), International Institute of Information Technology (IIIT), Hyderabad. My research is situated at the nexus of Cybersecurity, Machine Learning, and Healthcare Informatics. I am deeply committed to developing secure, intelligent, and resilient architectures for Medical Cyber-Physical Systems (MCPS). My work focuses on advanced authentication mechanisms, AI/ML security, and the integration of quantum-safe cryptographic frameworks to protect critical healthcare infrastructures.

With over 29 peer-reviewed publications and international research experience as an exchange researcher at NTNU and USN, Norway, I strive to bridge the gap between theoretical security and practical, real-world applications. Beyond research, I am a University Gold Medalist and an active reviewer for top-tier journals like IEEE Transactions on Information Forensics and Security (TIFS).

Research Guidance

I am incredibly fortunate to pursue my doctoral research under the supervision of Prof. Ashok Kumar Das .

Prof. Das is a Full Professor at IIIT Hyderabad and a globally renowned expert in Cryptography and Network Security. His mentorship provides the rigorous scientific foundation and innovative perspective necessary to tackle today's most pressing cybersecurity challenges.
