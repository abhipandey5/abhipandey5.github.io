About Me

Biography

Abhishek Kumar Pandey is a dedicated cybersecurity researcher, currently pursuing his PhD in Computer Science and Engineering at the International Institute of Information Technology, Hyderabad, under the guidance of Prof. Ashok Kumar Das. As an active member of the Center for Security, Theory, and Algorithmic Research (C-STAR), he is at the forefront of cutting-edge research.

With a research portfolio spanning over 20 published articles, his contributions to healthcare data security and management have been recognized by leading researchers worldwide. His current research focuses on machine learning-driven authentication systems and their applications in healthcare security.

Bringing over 4+ years of research and 2+ years of teaching experience, Abhishek is an active member of several scientific communities and serves as a reviewer for high-impact journals, including the top-ranked IEEE Transactions on Information Forensics & Security (TIFS).

Core Expertise

Cybersecurity & Cryptography

Authentication mechanisms, malware analysis, and secure protocol design.

Machine Learning

Developing intelligent systems for security applications and threat detection.

Health Informatics

Securing cyber-physical systems and ensuring data integrity in healthcare.
